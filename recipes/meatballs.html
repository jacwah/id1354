Meatballs

Recipe and picture from Arla.

Ingredients

  • 500 g minced meat
  • 0.5 dl breadcumbs
  • 1 dl cream
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped onion
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 ml black pepper
  • 2 dl butter

Directions

  1. Mix breadcrumbs and cream in a bowl. Let swell for 10 minutes.
  2. Add meat, onion, egg, salt and pepper. Mix until smooth.
  3. Shape the batter into small spheres and fry in butter for 3-5 minutes.
