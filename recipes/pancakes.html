Pancakes

Recipe and picture from ICA.

Ingredients

  • 6 dl milk
  • 2.5 dl wheat flour
  • 3 eggs
  • butter
  • a pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Mix flour and salt in a bowl.
  2. Whisk with half of the milk until smooth. Then add the rest of the milk and the eggs and mix.
  3. Fry approx. 1 dl of the mixture at a time in a hot buttered pan. Flip the pancakes to fry them on both sides.
  4. Serve with jam or syrup.
